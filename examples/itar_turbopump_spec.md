# Turbopump Assembly — Specification Sheet

**Part Number:** TPA-4421-R3
**Revision:** 3 (2026-02-14)
**Program:** Vulcan-III booster stage
**Parent System:** Vulcan-III liquid rocket engine (methalox, 1.2 MN vacuum thrust)

## Functional Description

Single-shaft, dual-impeller turbopump delivering liquid methane (fuel) and
liquid oxygen (oxidizer) to the main combustion chamber of the Vulcan-III
rocket engine. The assembly includes oxidizer and fuel centrifugal pumps,
a two-stage axial turbine, shaft seals, bearings, and integral tangential
inlet volutes.

## Performance Parameters

| Parameter                      | Value         | Units   |
|--------------------------------|---------------|---------|
| Design shaft speed             | 42,000        | rpm     |
| Oxidizer mass flow rate        | 320           | kg/s    |
| Fuel mass flow rate            | 87            | kg/s    |
| Oxidizer discharge pressure    | 310           | bar     |
| Fuel discharge pressure        | 340           | bar     |
| Turbine inlet temperature      | 780           | K       |
| Total shaft power              | 24            | MW      |

## Materials

- Pump impellers: Inconel 718
- Turbine blades: Mar-M-247 directionally solidified
- Shaft: AMS 5643 (17-4 PH stainless)
- Bearings: Si3N4 ceramic hybrid
- Seals: Carbon-graphite face seals with secondary labyrinth

## Intended End Use

Primary propulsion turbomachinery for the Vulcan-III first-stage liquid
rocket engine. The Vulcan-III engine is designed exclusively for the
Vulcan-III space launch vehicle. No commercial non-propulsion applications
are contemplated.

## Notes

This assembly has been purpose-designed for the Vulcan-III program.
Performance parameters (shaft speed, discharge pressures, turbine inlet
temperature) are selected to meet the specific throttle profile of the
Vulcan-III engine and cannot be met by general-purpose commercial
turbomachinery of equivalent size.
